# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Acceptable behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/piyushgupta344/structured-llm/issues) or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
